Built from surf, movement, and memory.

Wavstorm is the independent EDM project of Matthew Sorge, a San Diego artist shaped by the rhythm of the ocean and the rush of shared live moments.

The sound blends bright melodic hooks, festival-sized energy, and coastal atmosphere into remixes and edits made to feel immediate, emotional, and physical.
